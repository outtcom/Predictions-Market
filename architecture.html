Metaculus Superforecasters
Fetches 60 open binary questions. Extracts recency-weighted community prediction. Matches to Polymarket markets by text overlap (>60% Jaccard).
Threshold: |CP - market| > 5% → emit signal
2
LLM Ensemble
Tournament-grade prompt with base-rate anchoring, inside/outside view, uncertainty quantification. GPT-4o + Claude Sonnet 4-6 queried in parallel.
Median of models taken. Capped to top 10 markets by volume to manage cost/latency.

Manifold Cross-Platform
Fetches 300 top Manifold markets. Matches to Polymarket by text overlap (>65%). When prices diverge, the play-money consensus suggests mispricing.
Threshold: |P_manifold - P_poly| > 3% → emit arbitrage signal
